About the Event

A social coding competition that brings computer programmers to build or improve software/hardware ideas.

Participate for free and win cash prizes worth up to 20,000 in the monumental event of Muffakham Jah College of Engineering & Technology hosted by Computer Society of India & E-CELL, MJCET

Date: 19th January 2020 Venue: Muffakham Jah College Duration: 7 am to 10 pm

Lunch will be provided

Team Specifications:

  • Each team should comprise of 4-6 members including the team leader.
  • Each team must have at least one female team member..

Rulings:

  • The top 5 software teams from college will be nominated for Smart India Hackathon and their ideas will be submitted to SIH portal through college SPOC.
  • Teams are encouraged to think out-of-the-box and provide creative solutions to not only win prize money but also enroll their name into SIH.
